Scope note (parity): all five variants model the same journeys — clinic defaults, per-visit-type flow/notes override, room membership — with the same data (2 rooms, 4 visit options, MH flow override, SOAP/Progress notes). Not ported in any variant: patient preview, operating-hours editor, form library management, and the live flow/notes builders themselves (each variant indicates where today's builder would embed unchanged). The Current pane is the real app.

Recommendation (updated after team feedback that C reads as unintuitive): D and E are presentation answers, B and A are structural ones — and they compose. The strongest combination: B's catalog + inheritance underneath, with D's sentence cards as each visit type's summary view (click a phrase → the relevant section of B's editor). E is the best fit if the team prioritizes teaching new admins over dense management — it makes configuration self-explanatory at the cost of one-visit-type-at-a-time editing. C works better as a future admin "audit view" than as the primary UI. Your call.
